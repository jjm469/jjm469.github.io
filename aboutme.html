About Me

I chose my college major based on a video game character. Yup, it's true. During quarantine, I was obsessed with two games, Apex Legends and Rainbow Six Siege. And in both games, my two favorite characters were electrical engineers. That can't be a coincidence, right? I mean, who am I to ignore a sign like that? I have always loved and found computers interesting, so why not?

While my decision-making was undoubtedly dumb, I can say with 100% certainty that it was the best choice I could have made. I love what I do. Dissecting digital logic, building a processor, designing circuits, and learning to code has been an enriching experience; it has been challenging yet equally rewarding.

Regarding my future plans, I am proud to say I will be working as an FPGA Electrical Engineering intern at Raytheon Technologies over the summer! My short-term goals include getting involved with research or a project team. I will also continue my work as Corporate Director for IEEE and Corporate board member for SHPE.
